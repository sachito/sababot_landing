FAQ

How to install Sababot ?

Click on Add to Slack. Then select a channel or create a new one, add your teammates. Type /invite @sababot and you’re all set. Don’t worry, no spam guaranteed.

How does the free trial work ?

When you subscribe to premium plan, you’re automatically on a free trial period. You benefit from premium features for one full month.

Do you offer support ?

When you subscribe to premium plan, you benefit from a full support from our team. Just contact us at contact@iplus.io

How to switch to Premium ?

If you’re on a basic free plan, just say subscribe on a private conversation with sababot. He will take care of everything.

When can I unsubscribe ?

You’re charged every month, even when season is over. However, you can unsubscribe anytime.
